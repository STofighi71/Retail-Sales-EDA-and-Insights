# Column Names and their Descriptions

- **post ID** - Unique identifier for each social media post
- **platform** - Social media platform where the post was published (e.g., Instagram, TikTok, Facebook)
- **Post Type** - Type of content published (e.g., image, video, text, carousel)
- **Post Content** - Textual or descriptive content of the post
- **Post Timestamp** - Exact timestamp when the post was published
- **Date** - Calendar date of the post
- **Weekday Type** - Indicates whether the post was published on a weekday or weekend
- **Time** - Time of day when the post was published
- **Time Periods** - Categorized posting time (e.g., Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night)
- **Likes** - Number of likes received by the post
- **Comments** - Number of comments received by the post
- **Shares** - Number of times the post was shared
- **Impressions** - Number of times the post was displayed to users
- **Reach** - Number of unique users who saw the post
- **Engagement Rate** - Engagement rate calculated based on user interactions relative to impressions or reach
- **Audience Age** - Average age of the audience engaging with the post
- **Audience Group** - Age group category of the audience (e.g., 18–24, 25–34)
- **Audience Gender** - Gender distribution of the audience (e.g., Male, Female, Mixed)
- **Audience Location** - Country or region of the audience
- **Audience Continent** - Continent of the audience
- **Audience Interests** - Primary interests of the audience (e.g., Tech, Fashion, Fitness)
- **Camping ID** - Identifier for the marketing campaign associated with the post
- **Sentiment** - Sentiment score or label of the post content (e.g., Positive, Neutral, Negative)
- **Influencer ID** - Identifier for the influencer associated with the post (if applicable)
